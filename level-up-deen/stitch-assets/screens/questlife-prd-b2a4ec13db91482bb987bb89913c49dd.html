# QuestLife Product Requirements Document ## App Overview QuestLife is a gamified habit and lifestyle tracker. Users earn EXP and Level Up by completing real-life 'Quests' across various categories: Deen (Religious), Fitness, Finance, and Hydration. ## User Persona - Productive individuals who find standard trackers boring. - People looking to balance spiritual (Deen), physical (Fitness), and financial health. - Gamers who want to apply RPG mechanics to their life. ## Core Pillars 1. **The Daily Quest**: Your tasks for the day, presented as a mission log. 2. **Avatar Progression**: Your stats (Level, EXP, Rank) reflect your real-world habits. 3. **The Squad**: Social accountability and leaderboards. 4. **Holistic Tracking**: Deen, Fitness, Finance, and Water all in one place. ## Design Constraints - Mobile-first approach. - Bottom tab navigation for core actions. - Sidebar for deep-links on desktop. - High-fidelity components (Shadcn style).
